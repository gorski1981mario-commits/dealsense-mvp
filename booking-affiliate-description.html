Flight prices from Google Flights API (real-time pricing) 2. Hotel availability and pricing from Booking.com API 3. AI-powered matching to create optimal vacation packages 4. Smart filtering based on user preferences and budget 5. Direct booking links to Booking.com for seamless reservations Our Unique Value Proposition: • AI-Powered Recommendations: We use advanced algorithms to match users with hotels that best fit their preferences and budget, not just the cheapest or most expensive options. • Transparent Pricing: Users see complete package breakdowns including flight costs, hotel rates, and total vacation price with no hidden fees. • Dutch Market Focus: All content is in Dutch, prices in EUR, and we specifically target Dutch consumers departing from Amsterdam Schiphol and other Dutch airports. • Quality Over Quantity: We show users 3-5 carefully curated options rather than overwhelming them with hundreds of choices, increasing conversion rates. • Mobile-First Design: 70% of our users access DealSense via mobile devices, with a seamless responsive interface optimized for on-the-go booking.
